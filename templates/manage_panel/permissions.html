Permissions

Control which members can access the management panel and at what level.

Officer

Can manage content — announcements, trail work, members, sponsors, officers, dues. Cannot access Communications or Facebook settings.

Site Admin

All Officer permissions PLUS Communications setup (email/SMS credentials), Facebook settings, and Permissions management.

No access

Regular member — can log into their member dashboard but cannot access the management panel.
