How this works

Draw the trail by clicking points on the map. Each trail has a status (Open/Closed/Caution/Groomed/Planned) that colors it and a visibility (Public/Members/Both).

Trails appear on the public trail map and can be downloaded as GPX files for import into the Polaris Off Road app, OnX Offroad, Gaia, or any Garmin device.
