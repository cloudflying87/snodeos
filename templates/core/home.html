About the Club

Minnesota's Premier Snowmobile Club

Established in 1975, the Brainerd Snodeos was formed in response to community safety concerns about snowmobile usage in city limits. Today, we maintain over 107 miles of pristine trails throughout the Brainerd Lakes area.

Our 125+ volunteer members keep the trails groomed and safe throughout the season using two state-of-the-art Tucker Sno-Cats. We work closely with landowners, local businesses, and the community to promote responsible riding.
