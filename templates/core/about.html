Our Story

Established 1975

Welcome to the Brainerd Snodeos Trail System!

We proudly groom 107 miles of snowmobile trails in the Brainerd Lakes area, thanks to the dedication of over 50 volunteer members. Since 1975, when a group of snowmobilers formed the Brainerd Snodeos in response to a city request for safer snowmobiling, our club has grown to 125 members and evolved from hand-cut trails to operating two state-of-the-art Tucker Sno-Cats.

Maintaining the trails is a year-round effort involving tree clearing, bridge building, and signage. Our volunteers work hard to ensure safe and enjoyable rides for all. To help us keep trails smooth, please ride responsibly—stay right, avoid spinning your track, and take corners with care.

Thank you for supporting the Brainerd Snodeos. Follow us on Facebook for updates!
