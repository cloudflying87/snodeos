Our Story

Established 1975

The Brainerd Snodeos Snowmobile Club was founded in 1975 in response to growing safety concerns about snowmobile usage within city limits. A group of passionate riders came together with a shared mission: create a safe, organized environment for snowmobiling in the Brainerd Lakes area.

Over the past 50 years, we have grown from a small group of local enthusiasts into a thriving club of over 125 members. We maintain 107+ miles of groomed trails through partnerships with local landowners, the Minnesota DNR, and community businesses.

Our trails are 100% volunteer-maintained. Members dedicate countless hours throughout the season — from early fall preparations to spring wind-down — using our two state-of-the-art Tucker Sno-Cats to keep every mile in top condition.

We meet every third Monday of the month at 7:00 PM. New members are always welcome — whether you are a lifelong rider or brand new to the sport.
